C. TIME GRID (time-grid.tsx)
------------------------------------------------------------
Persisted in lib/time-tracking-store.ts (key cogs-timegrid-store).

1. Date navigation: "<" | "Today" | "Sat, Jun 20" | ">" | "Clear day"
2. Scope tabs: Activity (active) | Location | Mood | + (add scope) | delete
3. Pen palette (Activity scope defaults):
   Work (blue) | Rest (green) | Exercise (orange) | Social (pink) |
   Chores (purple) | Sleep (dark) | Erase | Edit pens
4. Manual range entry: From 09:00 AM → To 10:00 AM | "Fill range with selected pen"
5. Grid: rows 12:00 AM – 11:00 PM, columns = pens for painting minutes.
   Drag across cells or use range fill to log time.
6. Footer: "Untracked: 24h" when nothing logged.

Same component renders in compact mode inside the header Tracking dialog.
